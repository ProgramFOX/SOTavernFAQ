FAQ for The SO Tavern

What is The SO Tavern?

It's a room on Stack Overflow chat. You can find it here: The SO Tavern.

What is the topic of the room?

The topic of the room is pretty broad. Both general programming and Meta-discussion about Stack Overflow are on-topic, and discussion about other subjects such as chess are also allowed.

What is EditMonitor?

EditMonitor is a bot that runs in the room. It looks at recent suggested edits and reports them in the room of something looks wrong, for example: approved with 2 rejection votes, a link in the comment (usually spam), etc.

How can I contact the owners of the room?

To contact the room owners, ping @Sam, @Unihedron or @ProgramFOX in the room.

Which language do I have to speak here?

The room is an English-speaking room. Please try to use correct grammar and spelling, and avoid "text speak".
